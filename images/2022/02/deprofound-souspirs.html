Deprofound Souspirs - digital abstract art

Pools and canals reflected the many rooms and corridors of The Exhibition.

Verandas stuck out of the towers like floral crowns.

Swarms of butterflies covered the art pieces, feeding on the paint and marble and glass.

In a room with walls made of vine plants, under flickering vermilion lights, young couples danced in a shower of translucent turquoise liquid.

Abstract paintings drip-dripped to the floor, melting slowly under the sweltering, oxygen-rich artificial atmosphere.

The picture you see above is the only one I managed to rescue out of that inferno.
