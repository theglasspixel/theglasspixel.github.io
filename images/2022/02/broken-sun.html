Broken Sun - digital abstract art

This painting was found among the belongings of the late Bygmester Finnegan (he of the Stuttering Hand).

That great mind, alas, was unable to shape it into a final form deemed worthy of none but the greatest artistic spheres.

As it is, it may or may not be of concern to all those who take pleasure in casting doubt upon all those things regarding art, painting, visual dexterity, the appearance of a late afternoon sun on a pluvious day, the harmonics generated by the color yellow on a broken monitor plagued by digital-fatigue, or, obviously, the sound a cat makes while sneaking past an open window.

All these things are self-explanatory and there really is no point in going on and on about it. I think we can all agree on that, right?
