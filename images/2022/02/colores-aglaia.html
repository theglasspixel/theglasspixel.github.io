Colores Aglaia - digital art

Yesterday turned out to be a beautiful day. It was one of those damp and smoggy April days when everything is dark-rose and absinthe-green.

I stuck my head out of my apartment's window and looked at the jungle down there in the swamp, clouds of opalescent greenish vapor rising through the building's mirrored facades...

What does any of this have to do with the gorgeous piece of artistic achievement you see above this text? Well, I'm glad you ask, because--let me put it this way--you see... Once upon a time, there was this girl called Aglaia, and she was one of the three Graces, and she was very pretty, I think, so I did this digital painting, because, well, why not? Let's not think too much about things, okay?
