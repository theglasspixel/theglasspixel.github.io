Pilgrimage - digital abstract art

This wonderful piece of abstract digital art is meant to whip your inner-skull biomass into a frenzied biophilic ecstasy second only to the sublime nightmares of mid-stage environmental collapse.

As you can clearly see, this painting emanates the kind of powerful aromas one can find only in the most scintillating subterranean lakes.

Your plants will love it! That mold on your apartment's ceiling will grow like never before! Prospective sexual partners will climb through your windows and down your chimney!
