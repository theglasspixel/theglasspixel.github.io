Aftening - digital abstract art

So, I woke up today and immediately fell out the window and SPLAT! right on the sidewalk, people crawling all over me, complaining about the bleeding man lying all twisted on their path to very important things.

Anyway, when the ambulance finally arrived, they had to scrape me off the pavement.

I'm telling you, it's been a stressful day, but long story short, when I finally got home I turned on my trusty new computer and spent a few hours attacking this poor image. That got me feeling all warm and fuzzy, like a normal human being.

Oh well, tomorrow's another day I guess...
