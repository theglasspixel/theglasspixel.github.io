Corpolums - digital abstract art

This image was recuperated from the wreckage of an old sailor's limbic system.

As you can clearly see, it tells a story of underwater monsters, mermaids, nymphs lost to the infinite sea, ghost ships... You know, the usual stuff.

Given such a theme, it may be pertinent to ask why the complete absence of the color blue.

But that doesn't matter, really.

As the great bard once said: "Thanks eversore much, Pointcarried!"

As for me, the hesitant creator of this artistic achievement, I'm feeling very confused indeed.
