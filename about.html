Art as interested me since an early age. Art that is messy, chaotic, bewildering, ambiguous, that makes us feel alive, confused, doubtful, hesitant, and exhilarated. I think a work of art is like an encapsulated dream, and experiencing it is be like dreaming someone else's dream: an artist's dream.

I live and work in Portugal, near Lisbon and I've worked as a graphic designer for almost two decades now. For a while I dabbled in electronic music and photography, but for now I've settled with digital abstract painting as my main form of expression.
