New Painting Material

I found some painting material! Old-style, I mean. Nothing digital. So now I have a few crayons (aquamarine, aureolin, almond, maroon, and cadmium), a couple of notebooks, some old paintbrushes, and a few pieces of raw charcoal which I honestly don't know how to use, so I'll have to maybe waste a couple of paper sheets to try and get the hang of it.

I'm going to need a lot of green, though... Everything around here has been so overwhelmingly repossessed by nature that you could probably walk for kilometers without noticing the suburban buildings underneath all the greenery.

Then again, I really don't want to spend all my time scavenging for painting materials on old, long-abandoned apartments.

Plus, there's poison ivy everywhere. Huge, fleshy leaves. And they look very tasty too, the bastards.

Last night, I heard some dogs barking. That was certainly a surprise. Like pretty much everybody else, I thought that all pets outside the towers had either died from the same bacterial infestations that killed most people, or that they had all been eaten by spiders and other insects...
