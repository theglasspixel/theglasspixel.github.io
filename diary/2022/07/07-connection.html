Connection

un-reacting sensations in the brain
...
thoughts twisted, then spread open
...
misshapen, glass-like feelings
...
unconscious incoherence of images, sounds, movements
...
willpower overpowered by inner distortions,
...
phantom hands reaching consciousness
...
overwhelming fatigue weights like a tree
...
body motions like branches reaching for moonlight
...
unconscious clinging to the ground
...
sling-shot to the upper atmosphere, then back again to a state of numbness
...
painful exacerbation of cerebral micro-structures
...
circuits overheat
...
blade-like jolts of electricity disembody sense of reality
...
reality re-classifies
...
mental images coalesce, breakdown, re-classify
...
structures multiply perception
...
instant re-circulation of environs
---
