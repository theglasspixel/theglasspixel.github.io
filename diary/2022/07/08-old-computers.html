Old Computers

Drones have been flying over much more frequently these past days. Are they looking for me? I don't think I'm noteworthy enough for such special attention... A couple of them were beeping in strange patterns. Were they talking to each other? Why would they need to communicate by audible sound waves?

I am now squatting on an old---really old---neighbourhood with dark-stone houses. I saw what I think was once a water well, filled to the brim with oily greenish water. It had a disgusting smell to it. Really nauseating... It was on this well that I saw one of those feeding tubes from first-generation computers, still very healthy looking and crisscrossing its way from the water to a ruined basement.

As it turns out, this neighborhood is still fully networked. It's just that it's on a closed circuit, obviously feeding on the abundant water, rich with minerals and whatever else these computers need.

I am having a hard time connecting with these machines, though... I found a few badly damaged by water manuals, but that stuff is, I think, meant for technicians and very complex. Regardless, it shouldn't need a professional to simply turn it on and see if it still works... I have a feeling the machines are not happy to see me here...
