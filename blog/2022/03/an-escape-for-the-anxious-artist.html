An Escape for the Anxious Artist

Yes.
Please repeat after me:
om mani padme hum.
Again:
om mani padme hum.
Now keep saying these six syllables
in your mind,
over and over till nothing else
exists in there.
Till your thinking processes
become purified, clear,
perfectly in sync with the universe.
Say this mantra to yourself
over and over and over
and it will become your ego,
your flesh,
your substance.
It will become you,
it will move like you,
it will talk like you.
And then, finally,
you will be free,
you will attain enlightenment,
and the ground will slip out from under
your psyche,
and you'll contemplate infinity,
free from others,
free from the world,
free from yourself.
Om mani padme hum.
Om mani padme hum.
Om mani padme hum.
You are human,
but not for long.
Watch your hands and your feet,
your guts,
your heart,
om mani padme hum,
watch as it all rots
and disintegrates
and blends with the aether.
Om mani padme hum.
When you're finally done,
you will no longer exist.
Life will still be there,
pulsing inside the mind,
but the mind will be void.
You will be part of life,
completely,
but above all human activity,
a god in the primal chaos.
Om mani padme hum.
Any questions?
That will be 99,99.
Yes.
Now go away please.
