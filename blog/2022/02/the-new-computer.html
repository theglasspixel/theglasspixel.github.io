The New Computer

My new computer is humming away in the corner of the room. It's a brand-new machine I bought a few days ago and features all the modern niceties you might expect from such tech marvel. The pipes, of course, are now gray-lead free and no longer release those clouds of greenish, mildly hallucinatory vapor we all know and hate, being instead fully encapsulated in a tight mesh of beetle-shell black string fully integrated with the smart, algorithmic-evolving, networked, reverse-emiting embedded walls. (The walls are, obviously, nightly-fed with the daily-generated thoughts-and-dream report. Got to keep that data flowing!)

The old monitor (purple-phased and intermittently vermillion-decay radiating) is still fed by the local water system, thus connected to the ancient, legacy, interior walls. As I've written extensively in previous blog posts, this monitor still has the annoying habit of projecting images straight into your limbic regions, completely bypassing my so hard-earned neuro-psychic protections... Also, the water has been growing a nasty taste---way too sugary!---that makes my skin look like an old film, complete with flickering and annoying piano music that keeps me awake at night...

Anyway... stay tuned for updates!
